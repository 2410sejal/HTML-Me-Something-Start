Fascinating Facts About Roses

These timeless beauties are more versatile than you might think.

1. Roses Are One of the Oldest Flowers

It is no wonder that roses have been referenced in literature and music for centuries.
Archaeologists have discovered rose fossils that date back 35 million years.
Even more shocking, the oldest living rose is 1,000 years old.

2. You Can Eat Roses

Their petals are edible and rose water (made from soaking the petals in water)
is often added to jellies or jams, or is used as flavoring in Indian and Chinese dishes.
